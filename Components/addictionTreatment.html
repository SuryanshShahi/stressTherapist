Treatment For Food Addiction

Food addicts eat way more than required for proper nutrition and they continue to binge on things that make them feel good.This may have a number of negative physical, psychological, and social effects, including cardiovascular diseases, obesity, lack of self-confidence, depression, and social alienation. Due to the demand for induced emotions of joy, a food addict frequently resumes these harmful actions despite unintended consequences.
